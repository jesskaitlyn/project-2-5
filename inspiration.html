Goals

Open opportunities. We want to push students to become the best versions of themselves and allow them to explore all of their interests with our programs.

Origin

A brainstorm activity! I was give a prompt that asked me to brainstrom a non profit organization. I wanted to create something that would give back to my community and make students more passionate as they grow older.

Inspiration

My story. I struggled answering peole when asked, "What do you want to be when you grow up?" I could never make up my mind since there were so many options to explore. I hope to create this non profit in the future to solve that issue for some students.
